About AI

Artificial Intelligence is the simulation of human intelligence processes by computer systems. It involves the development of algorithms and computer programs that can perform tasks that usually require human intelligence, such as visual perception, speech recognition, decision-making, and language translation.

AI Applications

  • Self-driving cars
  • Virtual assistants
  • Image and speech recognition
  • Stock market analysis

AI Ethics

As AI becomes more advanced, questions arise about its ethical implications. Some concerns include job displacement, bias in decision-making algorithms, and the potential misuse of AI for malicious purposes.
